ABOUT US

Dr. Hema.D is a highly qualified Pediatrician and Neonatologist based in Bangalore with over 8 years of experience.

She completed her MBBS from Kempegowda Institute of Medical Sciences in 2012, followed by MD in Pediatrics from Rajarajeshwari Medical College in 2016. She then pursued a Fellowship in Perinatal Medicine from St. John's Medical College in 2018.

Currently, she is working as a NICU Consultant at Chinmaya Mission Hospital, managing a Level 3A NICU with 20 beds. Her practice focuses on delivering quality neonatal intensive care and pediatric treatment with a compassionate and evidence-based approach.

Experience

Dr. Hema.D completed her MBBS from Kempegowda Institute of Medical Sciences, Bangalore, in 2012. She pursued her MD in Pediatrics at Rajarajeshwari Medical College and Hospital, Bangalore, and completed it in 2016. To further specialize, she undertook a Fellowship in Perinatal Medicine at St. John's Medical College in 2018.

She worked in the Neonatology Department (NICU) at St. John’s Hospital from 2016 to 2019. She later served as Assistant Professor in the Department of Pediatrics at Rajarajeshwari Medical College & Hospital from June 2019 to November 2021.

Currently, Dr. Hema is a NICU Consultant at Chinmaya Mission Hospital, Bangalore, managing a Level 3A NICU with 20 beds, specializing in extreme preterm care, neonatal intensive care, and advanced procedures like UVC/UAC/PICC insertions and therapeutic hypothermia.
